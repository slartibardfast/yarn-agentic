The development of AI has been accompanied by ongoing ethical and societal concerns. Early debates focused on the potential for machines to achieve human-level intelligence and the implications of such a scenario. More recently, concerns have centered on algorithmic bias, data privacy, and the environmental impact of training large models. The environmental impact of training large models is particularly significant, with some estimates suggesting that training a single large language model can consume as much energy as hundreds of homes over a year.
